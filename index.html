We are Certified Probate Real Estate Specialists.
You can learn more about us and our services below or by calling one of our experts.

We assist administrators and executors with the sale of any real estate properties that the estate has to liquidate.

We work with investors and buyers to make this sale quickly, often within 30 days or less.
This can save you time and could save the estate from paying extra attorney fees, property taxes, and insurance.
Our Title Company partner can assist you in getting letters of testimentary, heirship affidavits and run a title search.

We can also help guide you to great subcontractors who can assist you with anything from re-keying the home to making any repairs .

Additionally we can assist you in interviewing an estate sale company, if you need to liquidate the contents of the home.
